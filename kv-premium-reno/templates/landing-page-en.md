## 8. FAQ Section (10 Questions)

### Q1: How much does it cost to renovate a condo?
**A:** It depends on size, style, and material choices. For reference, a full condo renovation in Klang Valley (800-1,200 sqft) typically ranges from RM 100,000 to RM 250,000. We offer free site visits and detailed quotations so you know every cost before work begins.

### Q2: How long does renovation take?
**A:** A condo renovation typically takes 8-14 weeks, terrace houses 12-20 weeks, and bungalows 6-18 months. The exact timeline depends on project complexity. We specify the delivery date in the contract and include a delay compensation clause.

### Q3: Are you CIDB certified?
**A:** Yes. We hold a CIDB G4+ license, and all our projects comply with Malaysia's Construction Industry Development Board standards. We also partner with registered architects for any structural modifications.

### Q4: How do you ensure transparent pricing? Are there hidden costs?
**A:** We provide a detailed quotation where every material and labour cost is clearly listed. After signing, the final cost will not exceed the quotation by more than 5% — unless you request changes. All variations are confirmed in writing with prior cost approval.

### Q5: Do you offer warranty?
**A:** Yes. All workmanship comes with a 12-month warranty covering: electrical & plumbing, painting, flooring, ceiling, and structural parts of custom furniture. We repair any construction defects free of charge within the warranty period.

### Q6: Can I just get the design and hire my own contractor?
**A:** Absolutely. We offer design-only services starting from RM 15,000, which includes floor plans, 3D renders, and a material specification list. You're free to engage any contractor to build from our designs.

### Q7: Do you provide smart home integration?
**A:** Yes! We offer smart home solutions including smart lighting, automated curtains, security cameras, and smart air conditioning. We partner with leading brands like Philips Hue, Daikin, and Hikvision to create a fully connected home.

### Q8: I'm overseas / in another state. Can we work remotely?
**A:** Yes. We offer remote design consultations via video call, with 3D renders reviewed online. During construction, our project management system provides daily photo and video updates — you can monitor everything without being physically present.

### Q9: What design styles do you offer?
**A:** We specialize in multiple styles: Scandinavian, Islamic Modern, Modern Minimalist, Modern Luxury, Japandi, and more. Your designer will present 2-3 concept options based on your preferences and property characteristics.

### Q10: How do I get started?
**A:** The easiest way is to WhatsApp us. Share your property address, approximate size, and what you have in mind. We'll respond within 24 hours and arrange a free site visit.
